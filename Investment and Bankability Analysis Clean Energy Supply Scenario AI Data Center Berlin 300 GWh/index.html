D — Debt Service Schedule
Senior debt is repaid over the loan tenor via annuity payments. The DSCR — the ratio of operating cash flow to total debt service — is the primary lender covenant. A minimum of 1.3x is standard for renewable energy project finance; anything below signals covenant breach risk and potential loan acceleration. Technologies shown in amber or red require immediate attention.

F — Stochastic Risk Assessment
Monte Carlo simulates 5,000 joint scenarios — simultaneously varying energy yield, CAPEX, OPEX, PPA price, and discount rate within their uncertainty ranges. The probability of positive NPV is the headline bankability figure: above 90% is considered low-risk by project finance lenders. P10 is the downside case; P90 is the upside. The width of the distribution reflects overall investment risk.

G — PPA Pricing Strategy
LCOE is the minimum viable PPA price — the floor at which the project breaks even on a lifetime discounted basis. Every unit of PPA above LCOE is margin. Selling as a bundled wholesale portfolio offers the offtaker supply diversity and price stability, justifying a premium over individual technology pricing. Technologies where PPA falls below LCOE are loss-making and must be repriced before financial close.
